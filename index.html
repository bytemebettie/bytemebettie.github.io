About Me

In 1999, while working as a Maid, I decided to start teaching myself how to code using a borrowed book, a borrowed computer, and on borrowed dial-up internet.

In 2001, I was offered my first programming job as a Logistics Analyst. My first project, I was tasked with developing a database-driven Night Vision Goggles inventory system for a US Navy Vision Lab.

The rest, as they say, is history.
